New Students

What to wear on your first lesson

Students should wear comfortable clothes that are easy to move in. For footwear, somthing with a back that won't easily fall off; ballroom shoes, ballet slippers or jazz shoes are acceptable. Hair should be pulled out of students’ faces and accessories (such as headbands, long necklaces, or bulky jewelry) that may become a distraction are discouraged with the exception of accessories students require to focus.
